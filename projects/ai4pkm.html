The Intelligent Second Brain

AI4PKM is a research-led cohort exploring the intersection of PKM (Personal Knowledge Management) and LLMs. We believe your notes should be a living ecosystem where AI helps you connect dots you didn't even know existed.

What We Build Together

  • Semantic Capture Systems: Moving beyond tags to automated, meaning-based organization.
  • Agentic Retrieval: Building AI agents that scan your personal knowledge base to answer complex questions.
  • Synthesized Insights: Using LLMs to find patterns and bridge ideas across your entire digital archive.
